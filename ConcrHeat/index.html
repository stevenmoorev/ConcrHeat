Interact with

A 1sq.m. painted wooden board laden with metal wires will showcase the heat wave phenomenon of a 100sq.km. area of the city of Montreal.

Learn More Progress

This project is made in CART411: Project Studio I

FASA is our main sponsor

The Fine Arts Student Alliance will be funding our project in total.

Thermochromic paint

Heat from the nichrome wire will change the colour of the city.

Overhead Projection

A satellite map of the city will be projected on the city, and trees will be projected on the hands of interactors.

Motion sensing

Photocells will track movement and control the electronic circuit with an Arduino.
